About Us

We are a competitive, high-performance agency operating out of the Labovitz School of Business and Economics. Our mission is to transform students into revenue-generating sales professionals.

Sales Summit Pitch Comp

Our flagship regional event. Students pitch a complex product to real-world executives for cash prizes, scholarships, and immediate job offers.

Pro-Bono Consulting

We operate as an internal collegiate agency, providing go-to-market strategies and lead generation training for our members via practical application.

Live Roleplay Workshops

Theory means nothing without practice. We hold weekly recorded mock-discovery and negotiation calls with senior members providing immediate feedback.

Mentorship Program

We don't leave success to chance. Our structured 1-on-1 mentorship program pairs incoming members with senior performers and industry professionals. You'll refine your cold-calling, negotiation, and elevator pitch under the guidance of someone who has already secured top offers.

  • Weekly mock discovery calls.
  • Resume and LinkedIn optimization.
  • Direct introductions to hiring managers.
